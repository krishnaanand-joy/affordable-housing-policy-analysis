# Privacy Notes

This repository is a public, sanitized case study.

## What is included

This repo includes:

- Aggregate survey findings
- Public-safe policy analysis
- A small chart-generation script
- Sanitized community-planning summaries
- General discussion of affordable housing, land use, zoning, adaptive reuse, and master planning

## What is not included

This repo does not include:

- Raw survey responses
- Individual respondent comments
- Names, emails, phone numbers, or addresses
- Private communications with public officials
- Internal county working drafts
- Non-public government documents
- Any data that could identify individual residents

## Why this matters

Housing policy work often involves resident input, local-government coordination, and community concerns. Public portfolio materials should show the research and analysis process without exposing private information or internal working materials.

## Public-release rule

This repository is designed to show the method, findings, and policy reasoning, not the private working files behind the project.
